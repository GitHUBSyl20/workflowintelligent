The prompting full day

One day to embed the method, work through advanced cases and align your team around generative AI.

Outcomes for your team
  • A reusable prompting framework applied to your real topics
  • Longer workshops on critical deliverables (quality, verification, tone)
  • Shared vocabulary and guardrails around data and compliance
  • A short 30-day adoption plan after the session
Key facts
Duration: 7h (breaks included) Format: on-site or remote Audience: operators, leadership, support functions Indicative fee: €900 excl. VAT / group Funding: OPCO-style options where applicable

Best when a half-day is not enough for your cases, or when several roles must converge on one method.

Sample agenda

Timing is adjusted to your sector and the deliverables you want to tackle live.

  • Morning — Framing & method: prompt structure, context handling, quality criteria, risk awareness.
  • Morning — Workshop: hands-on work on 2–3 business cases shared beforehand.
  • Afternoon — Going deeper: prompt chains, reusable templates, multi-tool nuances (based on your stack).
  • Afternoon — Workshop: group deliverable (document, synthesis, action plan) with group review.
  • Wrap-up: 30-day adoption plan, security checklist, shared resources.
