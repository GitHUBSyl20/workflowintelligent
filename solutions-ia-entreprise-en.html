    Voice agents for clinics: 24/7 availability, zero missed calls

    In aesthetic, dental, medical, or paramedical clinics, patients always ask the same questions: What time slots are available this week? Which treatments do you offer? What are the prices? How can I reschedule? The problem? These requests mostly happen in the evening, on weekends, or during peak hours. The result is calls on hold, missed appointments, and extra workload for the front desk.

    Solution deployed: the autonomous voice booking agent

    To solve this, I built an autonomous voice agent connected to your online calendar (Calendly, HubSpot Meetings, or Google Calendar). It answers automatically, understands the caller, handles common questions, and can book or reschedule appointments in real time, 24/7. The agent relies on AI tuned for natural conversations: understands everyday language, manages accents, asks clarifying questions, and routes intelligently (booking, information, urgencies, or human transfer).

    Technical stack

    • Twilio for call reception and handling
    • Vapi for voice orchestration (intents, behaviors, follow-ups, human handoffs)
    • Google Calendar for real-time availability sync
    • OpenAI API for understanding requests and intelligent routing

    Expected results

    • Up to 60% fewer simple calls (availability, hours, pricing)
    • +25% more appointments captured thanks to 24/7 coverage
    • Front-desk time saved: 1 to 2 hours per day depending on clinic size
    • Zero missed opportunities: instant response even outside office hours
    • Automatic contact collection for follow-ups and confirmations
    • Smoother patient experience, no waiting on the line

    A working demonstrator ready to replicate

    This voice agent already runs in a real-world environment and can be rolled out to any clinic, practice, or healthcare facility welcoming patients. If you want to measure the impact of an AI agent on call handling and appointment booking, I can help you deploy a fully connected functional prototype in no time.
