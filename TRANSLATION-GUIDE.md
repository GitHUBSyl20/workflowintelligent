## Usage Instructions:

1. Use Find & Replace in your editor
2. Replace each French phrase with its English equivalent
3. Verify context-specific translations manually
4. Keep technical terms and proper nouns unchanged

## Professional Translation Recommendation:

For legal pages (mentions-legales-en.html, conditions-generales-en.html), I strongly recommend hiring a professional translator familiar with French legal terminology.
