OPCO funding & confidentiality

A clear frame before any long commitment or sensitive data handover.

OPCO funding usually involves a partner training organisation and a dossier built with your HR team or OPCO contact.

  1. Introductory call to align need, audience and format (on-site, remote, in-house).
  2. NDA whenever data, mock-ups or confidential processes must be shared before detailed scoping.
  3. Agreement & OPCO pack with expected documents, timeline and follow-up checkpoints.
